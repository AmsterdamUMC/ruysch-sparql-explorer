Beneath the surface

Frederik Ruysch exposed anatomical structure through careful preparation. Rachel Ruysch revealed botanical order through observation and composition.

In the same way, SPARQL exposes the underlying structure of biomedical data: triples, relations, and joins that remain invisible in final results.

About this explorer

Ruysch SPARQL Explorer is a lightweight, read-only interface for exploring biomedical linked data. It emphasizes the relationship between visible query results and the structured foundations that make them possible.

The name honors Frederik Ruysch (1638–1731), anatomist, and his daughter Rachel Ruysch (1664–1750), whose work bridged science, structure, and careful observation.
